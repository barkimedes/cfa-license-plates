Specialty & Personalized Plates

You can use this page to order specialty and personalized license plates.
What is a specialty plate?
Normally, your license plate has the default plate design. A specialty plate is a license plate with a non-standard design, like the Denver Broncos logo or breast cancer awareness logos. Specialty plates will always have a fee associated with them -- that fee goes towards processing and the remainder goes towards the organization sponsoring the plate.
What is a personalized plate?
Normally, your license plate has a random set of seven (7) numbers and letters that is determined by the Division of Motor Vehicles. When your plate is personalized, you can choose the numbers and letters on the plate as long as (1) it hasn't been used in the past 5 years and (2) the Division of Motor Vehicles deems it appropriate. There is always a $55 fee associated with personalizing your plate.
How long does it take for my plates to arrive?
If you get a sequential specialty plate, it will take up to two (2) weeks to be delivered. If you get a personalized plated, it can take up to four (4) weeks. When your plates are ready, you will receive a postcard in the mail informing you to go to your local DMV office to pick up your plates.

Choose License Plate Text

Would you like to have a sequential or personalized plate? A sequential plate is a random set of seven (7) numbers and letters that is determined by the Division of Motor Vehicles. When you personalize your plate, you choose the text for your license plate. Note the following constraints on personalized plates:
  • The text must not have been used on an actively registered vehicle in the past five (5) years.
  • The DMV has the right to reject any text that it deems inappropriate.
  • Not every specialty plate allows personalization.
